Advanced Search

Use the advanced search to help you navigate the various search options available in the TDA. Search and discovery is only as good as the data available. Born-digital materials do not usually have expanded data such as county or subject terms/keyword. Instead, use the general search option to do a full-text search of documents.

Note: Search is based on a elimination method. Two contradictory terms will yield no results rather than the results of both options.
